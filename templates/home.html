How voting works

Follow these steps to participate in the university election.

1

Create account

Register with your full name, university ID and student email.

2

Confirm email

Open the link sent to your inbox to activate your account.

3

Wait for admin verification

Admins confirm your student status before you can vote.

4

Cast your vote

Log in, choose your candidate and submit a single secure vote.
