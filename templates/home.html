How voting works when you click

When you click the Vote button:

  • We check that you are logged in with your student email and verified by admin.
  • If you have not voted before, your choice is saved securely in the system.
  • Your candidate's vote count increases by one and the button changes to "Already voted".

Each verified student can submit exactly one vote for this election.
