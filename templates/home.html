How Voting Works

Follow these four simple steps to participate in the university election securely.

1

1. Register Account

Create an account using your full name, university ID, and student email for verification.

2

2. Verify Email

Click the unique link sent to your university inbox to successfully activate your account.

3

3. Admin Approval

Admins will confirm your student status and eligibility before granting you voting access.

4

4. Cast Your Vote

Log in, review the candidates' profiles, choose your selection, and submit your single, secure vote.
