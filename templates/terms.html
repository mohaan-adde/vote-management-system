Voting terms & conditions

These rules explain how the GSU online voting system should be used. By creating an account or casting a vote, you agree to follow these terms.

1. Eligibility

  • You must be a currently enrolled GSU student to register.
  • You must use your official student email and real university ID.
  • Each person may only create one account.

2. Account verification

  • You must confirm your email address before using the system.
  • An admin will verify your profile before you are allowed to vote.
  • Providing false information can lead to your account being disabled.

3. One person, one vote

  • Each verified student is allowed to cast exactly one vote per election.
  • You may not attempt to vote on behalf of another person.
  • Any attempt to manipulate the results will be investigated and may be reported to university administration.

4. Use of results

  • Results are for official university election purposes only.
  • Admins may export anonymised data to report turnout and winning candidates.

5. Privacy

  • Your password is stored securely by the authentication provider (Supabase).
  • Admins can see who voted and which candidate they selected only in aggregated form where possible.
  • We will not sell or share your personal data with third parties outside the university election process.

6. Support & contact

If you have any questions about these terms or your account, please contact the election team.
